Refund Policy

At Fresh Barrel, customer satisfaction is our priority. If you receive a damaged, incorrect, or quality-related product, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery.

Online Payment Orders

Refunds will be processed within 48 hours and credited to the customer's bank account within 5–7 working days, depending on the issuing bank.

Cash on Delivery Orders

Refunds for Cash on Delivery orders will be issued as Store Credit. The credited amount can be used for future purchases on Fresh Barrel.

Refund Eligibility

  • Damaged products received.
  • Incorrect product delivered.
  • Quality concerns reported within 24 hours.
  • Order cancelled before processing.

Non-Refundable Situations

  • Products not stored properly after delivery.
  • Complaints reported after 24 hours of delivery.
  • Issues caused by incorrect customer information.
